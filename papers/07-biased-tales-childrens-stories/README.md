# Biased Tales: Cultural and Topic Bias in Generating Children’s Stories

## Paper Info
- Title: Biased Tales: Cultural and Topic Bias in Generating Children’s Stories
- Authors: Donya Rooein, Vilém Zouhar, Debora Nozza, Dirk Hovy
- Venue / Year: EMNLP 2025
- Link: https://donya-rooein.github.io/files/biased-tales-demo/index.html

## Summary
This paper investigates how large language models encode
sociocultural biases when generating personalized children’s bedtime stories.
By systematically controlling sociocultural prompt attributes,
the authors analyze bias at multiple levels, including word choice,
character portrayal, and narrative context.
